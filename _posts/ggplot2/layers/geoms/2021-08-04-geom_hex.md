Divides the plane into regular hexagons, counts the number of cases in each hexagon, then maps the number of cases to the hexagon fill and convert them with ggplotly.


<pre class="mcode">
d <- ggplot(diamonds, aes(carat, price))
p <-  d + geom_hex()
</pre>


<pre class="mcode">
plotly::ggplotly(p)
</pre>
