Maps each level to an evenly spaced hue on the colour wheel and then convert them with ggplotly.






<pre class="mcode">
dsamp <- diamonds[sample(nrow(diamonds), 1000), ]
(d <- ggplot(dsamp, aes(carat, price)) + geom_point(aes(colour = clarity)))
</pre>


<pre class="mcode">
plotly::ggplotly(d)
</pre>
